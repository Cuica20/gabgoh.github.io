4 Years of Advice Animals
In 2006 Advice Dog flashed across the Internet like lightning, opening a whole new multicolored chapter of human communication - the advice animal. But in the Darwinian struggle for our collective attention, not all animals make it. Some die fast after exploding into existence. Some lead quiet lives. Other sit and wait, waiting for rebirth. Some keep going strong, living long and productive lives. Drawing on Jason Braughmerger's awesome reddit dataset, I have collected, for your viewing pleasure, all posts in /r/AdviceAnimal to Dec 2014 with over 50 upvotes. Enjoy!
