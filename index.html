4 Years of Advice Animals
I saw a beautiful spindle diagram in the natural history museum (it looked a little like this this or this) charting the evolution of species through time. I wondered if the same could be applied to memes on the internet. Perhaps they obeyed similar forces, living and dying in microclimates in our imagination. A long shot, perhaps. But here it is - a D3 visualization of Jason Braughmerger's meticulously mined reddit dataset showing all posts in /r/AdviceAnimal to Dec 2014 with over 50 up-votes. Enjoy!
